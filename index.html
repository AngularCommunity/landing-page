Angular Community

Open and independent, volunteer-run organization that aims to foster, grow and support the global community that is building technology experiences with the Angular platform.

Education

To educate those who want to learn about Angular. We help achieve this by promoting existing educational tools and supporting educational events for those who would like to create such events.

Learn more

Connection

To connect those who use Angular with their peers. We provide tools and guidance to ease barriers to entry for those who are looking to connect, whether online through a community discussion or in person at a meetup or other event.

Learn more

Assistance

To assist those who would like to take on greater leadership roles in the community. We share a firm belief that mentorship is one of the most powerful ways to grow a community, by creating new leaders who then become mentors themselves. Whether supporting first time speakers, organizers, or developers, we aim to give anyone interested in taking on more leadership paths to do so.

Learn more

Empowering

To empower existing community leaders in their efforts. We accomplish this first and foremost by listening to leaders around the world about their experiences building community. Then by creating tools and sharing ideas, we work with them to identify ways their success stories can be replicated in other places, and any pain points in community growth can be addressed.

Learn more
